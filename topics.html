My thoughts on the LGBT
My thoughts on the LGBT I feel are higher than some extreme conservative, but much lower for progessives. As an introduction, I would like to mention that I am a Reformed Anglican coming from a right winged perspective. I do believe being gay is a sin, but I would like you to read my perspective whether you are theologically left winged progressive Christian, not a Christian, or a theologically right winged Christian as I think this can be helpful to anyone.



The Divide from Denominations
Denominations have a lot of things good about them, but in their nature, they cause divide. Every Christian is united as brothers and sisters but their beliefs lead to separation in the religion. Do not be mistaken, I do think that we need denominations as it is very tough to believe all the same ideas, but as long as we are believing Jesus Christ is Lord, then the other ideas are not as fundamental (unless it's about the trinity, looking at JW's and Mormons).
